About Me

“A smooth sea never made a skilled sailor.” This quote has always reminded me of my journey so far throughout life. Coming from an extrememly poor upbringing and running into the hardships of being in the military I can completly agree that struggling makes you stronger. This has especially been true in my relationship with coding. Being thrown into programming on complex and poorly written programs with inadequate training created a sitaution were quick growth was necesary. I grew skills while programming in the Air Force but was often asked to complete tasks completly outside my scope of knowledge with little, and at sometimes no help. This developed into a lot of selfdoubt as a productive software developer. Once I became a civilian again I started my journey at a software bootcamp in Nashville. I decided to sharpen my skills in programming and I feel like it was possibly the best decision of my life. I have discovered I am more talented than I previously though and have rediscovered some of the love I had for programming.I am excited to continue this career pace and looking back I am glad the sea has always been storming for in times of calm you can see all the growth cleary.
